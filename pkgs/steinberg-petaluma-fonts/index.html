Petaluma is a Unicode typeface designed by Steinberg for its Dorico music notation and scoring application. It is compliant with version 1.3 of the Standard Music Font Layout (SMuFL), a community-driven standard for how music symbols should be laid out in the Unicode Private Use Area (PUA) in the Basic Multilingual Plane (BMP) for compatibility between different scoring applications. This package contains the Petaluma font. It is a Unicode typeface designed by Steinberg for its music notation and scoring application.
